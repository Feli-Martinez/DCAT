What is a dragon's zip?

When talking about a "dragon's zip" we refer to a compressed file that contains all the "spine" files of a dragon, made with the "Spine Editor". Such as their "skeleton", textures and animations.

I would love to teach you how to find them yourself but I cant because of some problems, so I've gone ahead and made a list with all the files so you can download whichever one you want.

If you don't have a background image dont worry! I've made some you can choose from

A small trick for choosing a good background is looking at the color wheel:

And picking a color thats more or less the opposite of your dragon's, for example, if you want to see/record the animations of the fragmented karma dragon, we can see most of it is a kind of gray-ish red, so a light blue background would be a good choice because of the nice contrast it makes with the dragon

If you have a dragon with many white or gray parts, like HCT, in my opinion beige is a good background color for them
